About Our Village

History & Introduction

Mandur is a progressive village located in the Gaganbawada Taluka of Kolhapur District. Known for its scenic beauty and agricultural heritage, our Gram Panchayat is dedicated to the holistic development of all its residents. We strive to provide transparent governance and efficient services.

Established with the vision of 'Adarsh Gaon' (Ideal Village), we focus on sustainable development, education, sanitation, and health for every citizen.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To ensure 100% literacy, sanitation, and employment opportunities within the village through effective implementation of government schemes.

Vision: A digital, green, and self-sufficient Mandur that sets an example for rural development in Maharashtra.
