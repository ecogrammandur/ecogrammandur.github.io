About Our Village

Establishment:
Gram Panchayat Mandur was established on 10th December 1957. It includes two revenue villages, 'Mandur' and 'Patilwadi'.

Geography:
Mandur village is situated on the banks of the scenic Kumbhi river. It is 25 km from Gaganbawada and 35 km from Kolhapur district.

Economy:
90% of the villagers rely on agriculture. Sugarcane is the main crop, along with rice and nachani in the rainy season.

Culture:
The village has two major temples, Khamba Devi (Patilwadi) and Rameshwar (Mandur), where festivals are celebrated with great enthusiasm.
